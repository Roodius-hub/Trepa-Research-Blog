1-Minute Round Mechanics

How Trepa's Flash Pools Work

1

Entry Opens

⏱ 0:00 - 0:30 (30 seconds)

Players submit their BTC price prediction using a slider interface. Everyone pays the same fixed entry fee.

Entry fee: 1 USDC (fixed for all players)
Submit your numerical prediction (e.g., $97,234)
No counterparty needed—pooled parimutuel model
Predictions locked once submitted
2

Market Closes

⏱ 0:30

Forecasting window closes. No more predictions accepted. Pool is locked.

All predictions are now immutable
Prize pool = total entry fees - platform take
30-second countdown to resolution begins
3

Resolution

⏱ 1:00 (exactly 60 seconds from start)

Actual BTC price is captured from oracle. Each player's error is calculated: |prediction - actual|.

Price oracle pulls real-time BTC data
Error calculated: e = |your_prediction - actual_price|
All errors are ranked from smallest to largest
Median error determines winner cutoff
4

Winner Determination

⏱ 1:00+

Players are split into winners and losers based on median error threshold.

Winners: Everyone with error below median
Losers: Everyone with error above median
Roughly 50% win, 50% lose (median split)
Entry fees from losers fund the prize pool
5

Payout Distribution

⏱ Instant

Prize pool distributed among winners proportionally to their accuracy weight. The closer you were, the bigger your share.

Winners get entry fee returned + proportional share of prize pool
Accuracy weight: inverse of error (closer = higher weight)
Losers get nothing (lose 1 USDC entry fee)
Precision Score (100-1000) assigned for leaderboards
6

Next Round Starts

⏱ Immediately

The cycle repeats. New forecasting window opens instantly for the next 1-minute round.

Continuous rounds, no downtime
Play as many rounds as you want
Accumulator bonuses for winning streaks
Leaderboards track cumulative Precision Score

The Complete Loop

Trepa compresses the entire prediction market cycle—forecast, resolve, distribute—into 60 seconds. This speed is only possible because of the pooled parimutuel structure. No orderbook matching. No AMM slippage. Just: predict, resolve, get paid.

60s Full round cycle
30s Forecast window
1 USDC Entry fee per round
~50% Win rate (median split)
